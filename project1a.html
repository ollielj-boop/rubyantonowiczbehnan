2025 Residencies: Home Live Art and Cie Natalie Beasse

On the Reignite Residency with Home Live Art, collaborator Yui Yamamoto and I developed a new experimental sonic performance, with a public sharing and feedback session. Over the course of five days, we focused on investigating the use of our voices in composition, examining vocal techniques, sustaining single words to create harmony, and exploring phrases to build rhythm. We also examined spatial and immersive elements, including our movement and the positioning of the sound sources in relation to the audience.
