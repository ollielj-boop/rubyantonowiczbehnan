Work in Progress 2024-25: Broadworks by Hive Creates, Somers Gallery and Embassy Gallery

Requiem in Progress is a multidisciplinary performance duo by Yui Yamamoto and Ruby Antonowicz-Behnan, collaborating to explore the shared experience of grief. Rooted in sound-based performance, the project encompasses theatre, experimental performance, and installation with the aim of opening new perspectives on private and collective loss, examining how grief is experienced, voiced, and shared within performative space.
